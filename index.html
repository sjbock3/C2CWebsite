Welcome to C2C Industries, LLC

Who We Are

C2C Industries is a state of the art CNC machine shop which specializes in CNC Turning and CNC Milling. We are located in Chino, California, in a modern, secure 21,000 square feet building. We operate 2 shifts from 6 AM until 12:30 AM.

What We Stand For

At C2C, we take pride in partnering with our customers to assure we meet all of their needs. We focus on competitive pricing, on-time delivery, and high quality in order to ensure the best possible service to our customers.

Quality

C2C Industries consistently strives for excellence in quality of our product and service, which will create value for our customers. We comply with customer requirements and continually strive to improve the effectiveness on the QMS.
