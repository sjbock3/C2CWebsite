Overview

At C2C, we use our own automatic band saws that can cut up to 14 inches in diameter and automatically feeds material so you can set it and walk away. By using our own saws, this allows us to purchase material at a lower price, which is passed on to our customers. This also cuts out a 3-5 day lead time and allows for next-day deliveries. We work closely on partnering with our material vendors to ensure the lowest prices, fast deliveries, and that material is in stock. We have a wide variety of materials on our own racks to serve our customer when a job needs to be expedited.
