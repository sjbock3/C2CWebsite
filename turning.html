Overview

At C2C, our turning department is made up of state-of-the-art CNC machines. We house 18 machines with capabilities to barfeed up to 12 feet in length and 4 inches in diameter. We also have chucking capabilities up to 21 inches in diameter and 26 inches in length. Some of our machines have mill and turn capabilities to run complex parts with minimal handling time involved. Our lathes operate with high quality tooling and work-holding devices to speed up cycle times. This ensures we produce the highest quality parts at the lowest possible cost.
