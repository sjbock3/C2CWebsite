About C2C Industries

In 1954 Jakob Bock emigrated to the United States from Germany. He apprenticed at a machine shop in Southern California and started his own machine shop in 1973. While in high school Jack and his brother Roy apprenticed under their father and they eventually took over the family business in 1988. In 2006 Jack left the family business to pursue his dream of owning his own shop. This was the beginning of C2C. Today, C2C has a team of over 35 employees who run two shifts from 6AM to 12:30AM. We incorporate a modern 21,000 square foot facility with state of the art CNC machines. We are thankful for God's blessings everyday and wish you all the best from C2C.
